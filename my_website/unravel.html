Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is a psychological horror/thriller anime following normal boy Kaneki Ken and how he becomes tangled up within the ghoul world and the never ending fight between humans and ghouls. Both willing to do whatever it takes to survive.The plotline of Tokyo Ghoul is mainly spotlighted on the main character Kaneki Ken after he gets into an accident ends up having ghoul organs transplanted in him and essentially becomes half human/half ghoul. We get to see Kaneki struggle intensely with who he is now and how he tries to tame his ghoul instincts. He defiantly goes through the entire process of denial when he forces himself to eat normal human food but cannot keep it down at all. Throughout the entirety of season one you follow Kaneki’s development, from him learning how to make coffee at Antique to him essentially accepting who he is. Although, Kaneki’s story shines the most in Tokyo Ghoul other themes within the plot also come into play such as hat is human, what is humane and is there a definite line between good and evil or can in become blurred?
